Biography

Aditya Kulkarni (Graduate Student Member, IEEE) received the M.Tech. degree from the National Institute of Technology (NIT) Goa, India. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Dharwad, India. His research interests include cryptography, cybersecurity, machine learning, and deep learning. He received the IEEE/ACM COMSNETS 2024 Best Graduate Forum Honorable Mention Award .
